# Janga Design System

## Palette
- **Background**: `hsl(240, 10%, 4%)` (Deep Obsidian)
- **Surface**: `hsl(240, 10%, 8%)` (Dark Charcoal)
- **Primary**: `hsl(270, 100%, 65%)` (Electric Purple)
- **Secondary**: `hsl(190, 100%, 50%)` (Neon Cyan)
- **Accent**: `hsl(330, 100%, 60%)` (Pink Glo)
- **Text Primary**: `hsl(0, 0%, 100%)`
- **Text Secondary**: `hsl(240, 5%, 70%)`

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: 'Outfit', sans-serif
- **Heading**: 'Outfit', sans-serif (Semi-bold to Bold)

## Effects
- **Glassmorphism**: `backdrop-filter: blur(12px); background: hsla(240, 10%, 12%, 0.7); border: 1px solid hsla(0, 0%, 100%, 0.1);`
- **Gradients**: `linear-gradient(135deg, hsl(270, 100%, 65%), hsl(190, 100%, 50%))`
